Help the Poor!

Use the UP and DOWN arrow keys to move the your player (blue) towards

each gray square (poor person). Once you reach your intended location,

press the SPACE bar to give them money. Make sure you give everyone

money before the time is up!

Poverty is not having the resources for basic necessities such as water, food, and shelter. Both poverty and hunger are important issues that are affecting more and more people around the globe. Around 690 million people go to bed hungry every night.
