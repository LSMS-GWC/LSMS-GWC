COVID-19

LSMS Coders

In this game, you are walking in a store, during the COVID pandemic! Even though many are getting vaccinated, we must still follow social distancing and masking. You are denoted by a yellow square, and others are denoted by red squares. Use the up/down arrows on your keyboard to stay far from others "walking" in the store, and use the right arrow to reach the end of the store. Good luck!

The coronavirus is a new disease that was discovered in 2019. It is a respiratory disease and everyone is at risk, especially those with underlying medical conditions are more susceptible to severe illness.
